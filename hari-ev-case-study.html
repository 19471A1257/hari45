UX Case Study

Hari EV — Charging & Vehicle Experience

Designing a seamless EV app for charging access, battery insights, and vehicle health.

Role: UX & UI Designer
Duration: 4 Weeks
Tools: Figma · FigJam · Google Forms · Google Docs · Photoshop · Illustrator
Mobile UX Service Design Design Systems Usability Testing

Overview

Hari EV is a mobile platform that helps EV owners quickly discover compatible charging stations, plan reliable routes, and understand battery health—reducing range anxiety and decision friction.

Scope: Research → Competitive Analysis → IA → Wireframes → Visual Design → Hi‑Fi Prototype

Problem & Goals

Challenges

  • Limited visibility into reliable, nearby charging stations
  • Complex battery information and unclear health indicators
  • Fragmented support for vehicle monitoring and maintenance

Goals

  • Real‑time charging availability and compatibility
  • Clear, human‑readable battery status and guidance
  • Intuitive navigation and EV‑friendly route planning

User Research

Surveys and interviews revealed recurring themes: charging accessibility gaps, uncertainty around battery health, and demand for a cleaner, more guided UI.
